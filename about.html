About

In 2017, my friend Erik invited me to my very first wine tasting at a local wine shop. I knew very little about wine, but that evening opened the door to a world of remarkable people, unforgettable wines, and lifelong friendships. Nearly a decade later, both that shop and Erik remain an important part of my life.

The best local wine shops do more than sell wine—they become places where conversations begin, friendships grow, and communities gather.

This directory is a celebration of those places: the local retailers, owner-operated shops, and neighborhood wine stores that make wine more personal. Whether you're searching for tonight's bottle, planning a trip, or discovering wine for the first time, I hope you'll start with a local wine shop.

Thanks for stopping by,

Will
